# Comprehensive Question List for Freelance Website Developer

## 📋 Project Overview & Goals
1. What is the primary purpose of your website? (Business, Portfolio, E-commerce, Blog, etc.)
2. What are your top 3 business goals for this website?
3. Who is your target audience? (Age, location, profession, interests)
4. What action do you want visitors to take on your site? (Contact, Purchase, Subscribe, Download)
5. Do you have any existing website? If yes, what do you like/dislike about it?
6. What makes your business/product/service unique?
7. Do you have any competitors? Which competitor websites do you admire?

## 🎨 Design & Branding
8. Do you have an existing brand identity? (Logo, colors, fonts, style guide)
9. What is your preferred color scheme? (Provide hex codes if available)
10. What typography/fonts do you prefer?
11. Show me 3-5 websites you like the design of and explain why
12. Show me 3-5 websites you DON'T like and explain why
13. What mood/emotion should the website convey? (Professional, Fun, Luxurious, Modern, etc.)
14. Do you have any specific imagery preferences? (Photos, illustrations, icons)
15. Should the design be minimalistic or detailed/content-rich?

## 📱 Features & Functionality
16. What pages/sections do you need? (Home, About, Services, Portfolio, Contact, Blog, etc.)
17. Do you need any special features? (Contact forms, Booking system, Payment gateway, User login, etc.)
18. Do you need e-commerce functionality? (Product catalog, Shopping cart, Checkout, Inventory management)
19. Do you need a blog/news section? (Content management system)
20. Do you need multilingual support?
21. Do you need integration with third-party services? (CRM, Email marketing, Social media, Analytics)
22. Do you need a member/user registration system?
23. Do you need search functionality?
24. Do you need any animations/interactive elements?
25. Do you need video/audio integration?

## 📊 Content & Assets
26. Do you have existing content? (Text, images, videos, documents)
27. Who will provide/write the content for the website?
28. Do you need content creation/copywriting services?
29. Do you have high-quality images/videos or need stock photos?
30. Do you have testimonials/case studies to include?
31. Do you have legal documents? (Privacy policy, Terms of service, Cookie policy)
32. Do you have social media profiles to link?

## 🔧 Technical Requirements
33. Do you have a preferred technology stack? (WordPress, React, Laravel, etc.)
34. Do you need mobile responsiveness? (Desktop, Tablet, Mobile)
35. What browsers/devices must be supported? (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge, iOS, Android)
36. Do you need SEO optimization?
37. Do you need speed/performance optimization?
38. Do you need security features? (SSL, Firewall, Regular backups)
39. Do you need analytics integration? (Google Analytics, Hotjar, etc.)
40. Do you have hosting/domain preferences?
41. Do you need email setup? (Professional email addresses)

## 📅 Timeline & Budget
42. What is your expected launch date?
43. Is there a specific event/season driving this timeline?
44. What is your budget range for this project?
45. How do you prefer to handle revisions/feedback?
46. How many rounds of revisions are included?
47. What is your preferred communication method? (Email, Phone, Video calls)
48. How often would you like progress updates?

## 🚀 Post-Launch & Maintenance
49. Do you need training on how to update/maintain the website?
50. Do you need ongoing maintenance/support services?
51. Who will handle content updates after launch?
52. Do you need regular backups?
53. Do you need security monitoring?
54. Do you need performance monitoring?
55. Do you need SEO/content updates?

## 🎯 Success Metrics
56. How will you measure the success of this website? (Traffic, Conversions, Sales, Leads)
57. Do you have any specific KPIs to track?
58. Do you need conversion tracking setup?
59. Do you need A/B testing capabilities?

## 💼 Business Context
60. What is your company's story/mission?
61. What are your company's values?
62. Who are the key team members to feature?
63. What is your company's unique selling proposition (USP)?
64. What problems does your business solve for customers?
65. What are your long-term business goals?

## 🔍 Additional Discovery
66. What concerns/fears do you have about this project?
67. What has been your experience with previous developers/agencies?
68. What is your decision-making process for this project?
69. Who else needs to be involved in approvals?
70. Is there anything else I should know about your business/project?

## 📝 Follow-up Questions (Based on responses)
71. Can you share examples of content you like?
72. Can you provide access to current analytics (if existing site)?
73. Can you share your brand guidelines/documentation?
74. Can you introduce me to your team members involved?
75. Can we schedule a call with all stakeholders?

## 🤖 For Chatbot Implementation:
**Initial Questions (First 5-10):**
1. What type of website do you need? (Business, E-commerce, Portfolio, Blog)
2. What is your industry/business type?
3. Do you have an existing website?
4. What is your timeline for launch?
5. What is your budget range?
6. Do you have brand materials (logo, colors)?
7. What are your top 3 goals for this website?

**Progressive Questions:**
- Based on answers, ask more specific questions
- Use conditional logic (if e-commerce → ask about products, payment, etc.)
- Ask for examples/links to reference sites
- Clarify technical preferences based on their needs

**Final Questions:**
- Contact information
- Preferred communication method
- Next steps/availability for detailed discussion
